Aynage Child and Family Development Organization is a dedicated NGO working to uplift children and families in Ethiopia through education, health, nutrition, livelihood, and youth development programs. Based in Kibet Town, Silti Woreda, it operates across eight rural and urban kebeles. Established in 1999 and fully operational by 2001, it was formerly known as Christian Children’s Fund. Re-established in 2008 as Silti-Aynage Child and Family Development Association, it now operates under its current name with a 13-member board and 22 staff members.
ACFDO exists to bring about an environment where the educational, health, psychosocial and economic needs of deprived, excluded and vulnerable children are met by ensuring quality education, enhancing household livelihood and youth engagement; and improving quality health services.
ACFDO aspires to see all deprived, excluded and vulnerable /DEV/ children become productive, confident and responsible citizens, parents and leaders of tomorrow that directly involve and contribute for the development of the country.
