Aynage Child and Family Development Organization is a well-established NGO dedicated to improving the lives of children and families in Ethiopia through education, health, nutrition, livelihood, and youth development programs. Located in the Central Ethiopia Region, Silte Zone, Silti Woreda, Kibet Town, Aynage operates across eight kebeles, both in rural and urban areas. With a mission to empower communities and create sustainable change, Aynage has impacted thousands of lives, fostering development and resilience in the regions it serves.
Aynage Child and Family Development Organization exists to bring about an environment where the educational, health, psychosocial and economic needs of deprived, excluded and vulnerable children are met by ensuring quality education, enhancing household livelihood and youth engagement; and improving quality health services.
Aynage Child and Family Development Organization (ACFDO) aspires to see all deprived, excluded and vulnerable /DEV/ children become productive, confident and responsible citizens, parents and leaders of tomorrow that directly involve and contribute for the development of the country.
